Keto Tacos

    taco seasoning
    2lbs ground meat
    1 cup cheese
    1/3 TBSP avacado oil
    salt to taste
    pepper to taste
    low carb tortilla
    sour cream
    taco sauce

Instructions:

    Stove on 7-8 heat
    Salt and pepper ground meat in pan
    drain ground meat place ground meat back in pan and seasoning and mix
